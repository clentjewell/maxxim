Rivals

Global players (Sword, Hinge, Kaia) are funded but imported and remote, weaker on local payer and surgical fit. Local physio networks and in-house insurer programs are trusted or safe but lack platform leverage. The real competitor is delay. Our response: own Australian, clinician-founded, payer-aligned MSK coordination, backed by documented local proof.

White space: local fit + full pathway + proof
