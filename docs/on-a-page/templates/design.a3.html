Strategy on a line

The clinician-founded MSK pathway platform that lowers Australian payers' claims cost and improves member outcomes by coordinating the full pathway, not one episode.

Proven, not promised — roughly 3x ROI on nib / Honeysuckle Project Mobilise. Why, then Result, then Method: every buyer-facing asset opens on the belief before it names the platform.

Brand

Essence: care beyond the clinic door, so people can simply Be. Archetype: Sage plus Caregiver core, expressed now with Hero and Outlaw courage, growing toward a Jester north star. Voice: direct, credible, grounded, warm. Tagline: "The whole pathway, not one episode."

Primary — vivid orange
Secondary — deep blue / cobalt
Neutral dark — near-black
Neutral light — off-white

Type: display face (campaign headlines) · clean geometric sans (logotype/UI) · legible sans (body). Exact hex, CMYK/PMS and typefaces locked at brand-identity stage.

Patient

Business model
  • Revenue: partnership/licensing (Stryker, ~AUD 550k across a 2+3-year term), per-cohort program fees (~AUD 1,200), telehealth/platform fees (~AUD 49/mo, ~AUD 195 patient fee).
  • Pricing posture: one procurement-safe card per buyer type — indicative, pending Ali Stokes validation and leadership sign-off.
  • Unit economics: referenced gross margins ~80% prevention/CDMP, ~70% surgical — to hold as service-delivery cost scales.
Messaging

Pillars: proof before promise, clinician-built credibility, Australian differentiation, whole-pathway coordination. Voice: plain over technical, evidence-led over hype. Do: "roughly 3x ROI" — named and sized to evidence. Don't: "digital physio", "just an app", AI-hype, generic wellness language.
