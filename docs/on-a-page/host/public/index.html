Killer Questions — the 5 truths
  1. BTC has done the hard part: real product, real clinical authority, real market proof. The question is not legitimacy — it is packaging.
  2. The growth bottleneck is conversion architecture, not product. BTC needs cleaner translation of what already exists, not more ideas.
  3. Different buyers want different BTCs — telling them all the same story at once is the core failure mode.
  4. BTC is over-dependent on one person, Ali Stokes, for narrative, proposals and close. Sales infrastructure must reduce that risk.
  5. Pricing clarity is the biggest unlock. The product is ahead of the pitch; interest does not close without procurement-safe packaging.
